# Business Insights

## Key Findings

- Total customers: 8000

- Male customers: 3900
- Female customers: 3805
- Other: 295

- Middle Age customers are the largest segment with 2853 customers.

- Premium customers: 1749
- Non-Premium customers: 6251

- USA has the highest premium customer count with 551 premium customers.

- Spain has the highest premium conversion rate at 24.27%.

- Germany has the highest email verification rate at 93.33%.

- Customer registrations remained stable:
  - 2020: 4012
  - 2021: 3981

## Recommendations

- Focus premium membership campaigns on USA, UK, and Germany.

- Increase premium conversion in countries with high customer counts but lower premium percentages.

- Maintain strong email verification practices.

- Target Middle Age customers through personalized marketing campaigns.
